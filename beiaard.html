Beiaardcantus

Organized by LOKO

On the 14th of May LOKO will organize the 8th edition of the Beiaardcantus (or carillon cantus). The carillon will be ready to guide the biggest cantus of Belgium! Once again, 4,000 students from Leuven will descend upon Ladeuzeplein for an evening of song, fellowship and fun. An experience you will undoubtedly want to cross off your bucket list.

WHEN:
14 May 2025, 6 PM - 10 PM
WHERE:
Monseigneur Ladeuzeplein, 3000 Leuven, Belgium
