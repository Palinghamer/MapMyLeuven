About Map My Leuven

A Digital Humanities initiative

Our Mission

Map My Leuven aims to put Leuven's events more prominently on the map, creating a centralized platform where both citizens and tourists can discover everything happening in the city.

Whether you're a long-time resident, a student new to the area, or a visitor exploring Leuven for the first time, our interactive map and comprehensive event listings make it easy to connect with local culture, entertainment, and community gatherings.

About the Project

Map My Leuven was developed by five enthusiastic students as part of the Web Information Systems course in the Advanced Master in Digital Humanities program at KU Leuven.

Our platform features:

  • Interactive map of events throughout Leuven
  • User ability to add events to the map
  • Weather integration to help plan your outings
  • Category and date filters to find exactly what you're looking for
  • Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go access
