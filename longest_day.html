The Longest Day

Organized by Shopping in Leuven

This year it will be a true spectacle again with The Longest Day, the Musical on Saturday 21 June. Expect a party full of colour, music, dance and joy that will get the city moving. Stroll along the shops, enjoy the flea market prices, relax on the summer terraces and be surprised by the magic of musical. This is perhaps the best shopping event of the year. Shopping on Sunday? There will also be fun entertainment in the city on 22 June. You can check the opening hours of your favourite shops via their social media channels.

WHEN:
21 June 2025, 10 AM - 22 June 2025, 6 PM

Leuven center, 3000 Leuven, Belgium
