Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Map My Leuven

What is Map My Leuven?

+

Map My Leuven is an interactive platform that helps citizens and tourists discover events happening in Leuven. Our platform features an interactive map, comprehensive event listings, and tools to help you plan your visits.

How do I find events on the map?

+

You can access our interactive map from the homepage or by clicking on "Event Map" in the navigation bar. From there, you can see all events plotted on the map. You can filter events by category, date, and cost to find exactly what you're looking for.

Can I add my own event to Map My Leuven?

+

Yes! We welcome event submissions from the community. To add your event, you'll need to create an account and submit your event details through our online form. All submissions are reviewed before being added to the map.

Is Map My Leuven available as a mobile app?

+

Currently, Map My Leuven is only available as a mobile-responsive website that works well on all devices.

How does the weather feature work?

+

Our weather feature provides current weather conditions in Leuven and recommendations on whether you'll need a jacket. This helps you prepare appropriately for outdoor activities.

How can I contact the Map My Leuven team?

+

If you have questions, suggestions, or feedback, you can reach us via email at contact@mapmyleuven.be. We welcome your input and are committed to improving the platform based on user needs.
