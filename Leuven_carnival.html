Leuven Carnival

Organized by City Leuven with Children and City Leuven

The international carnival procession of the 'Order of the Pietermannen' moves through the city centre with floats, dancing girls, music, feathers, masks and colourful figures. The procession leaves at 15:11 from the Sint-Jacobsplein and continues along: Kruisstraat Brusselsestraat Franz Tielemanslaan Dirk Boutslaan Mathieu de Layensplein Ferdinand Smoldersplein Rijschoolstraat Sint-Maartensdal Jean Baptiste Van Monsstraat Diestsevest Martelarenplein Bondgenotenlaan Rector De Somerplein Margarethaplein The procession ends at 17:11 with a free party in the Rijschool hall. All details via our website.

WHEN:
7 March 2025, 3 PM - 12 AM
WHERE:
Leuven center, 3000 Leuven, Belgium
